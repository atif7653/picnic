2.Kick The Can

Header
KICK THE CAN RULES

1. Kick the Can is a game that has been popular since the 1930s.
2. It combines tag, hide and seek, and capture the flag into one exciting game
3. Any age group can enjoy this game, and it can be played with as few as 3 or over 20 players.
4. [1] To get your game of Kick the Can started off right, you'll need to organize players and the field, doing things like establishing boundaries and setting up a jail for caught players.
5. After that, playing the game is relatively simple, and there are variations you can use to keep this game fresh.

3.Four square

Header
FOur Square Rules

1. Play begins when the person who just entered the four square—in square D or 4— serves the ball.
2. The ball can only bounce once in any square.
3. Each player must hit the ball with any part of their hand into an opposing player’s square after it has bounced only once in their own square.
4. If a player makes a mistake, every player must rotate—D to C, C to B, B to A, and A to the line–and a new player enters into square D.
5. Mistakes include:
.The ball lands on a line. .The ball goes out of bounds. .The ball bounces twice in one square. .A player catches or holds the ball. .The ball is returned before it is allowed to bounce. .After players rotate, play begins again with a serve from the new player in square D.

Jump-Rope and Double Dutch Rules

3. “Let me go again!” she says.

5. Double Dutch — a game where two players turn a pair of ropes like eggbeaters, while a third jumps between them — is making a comeback, and this time it’s not just for kids.

5.Capture The Flag

Header
Capture The Flag Rules

1. This classic outdoor game for kids requires six or more people and two flags (pieces of fabric or paper in different colors).
2. Start by dividing the available outdoor space into two adjoining courts that are roughly the same size and large enough to make the game fun (but the size can vary depending on available space).
3. Split the players into two teams, and have each place a flag near the back of their court.
4. The object of the game: Players must dash into the opposing team's court and steal their flag. A player becomes "out" if they get tagged, and they win the game if they successfully capture the flag.
