Five Games For Men

1. Four Square Volleyball

Header
Four Square Volleyball

This is a twist on the classic game of four square. The object of the game is to hit the ball back and forth between players, like a volleyball, without letting it touch the ground.

RULES :

Both Four Square and Volleyball are common recess games for kids in kindergarten through high school. The ideas of the games are similar--you must successfully pass the ball to the opposite player. In volleyball, the players set, bump, and spike the ball over a net to another team. On the other hand, Four Square players hit a bouncing rubber ball on the ground and bounce it to one of three other players. .

2.Kan Jam

Header
Kan Jam

Kan jam is a fun party game for adults that can be played with teams of two. The aim of the game is to throw or deflect a Frisbee into a trash can or bin. The team that scores the most points wins the game. This outdoor game can be an easy DIY project or you can purchase Kan Jame equipment online.

RULES :

Set-up Set up your Kans opposite from one another. (50 feet apart for recreational and competitive play).Choose Teams 2 teams. Each team member stands behind the kans on opposite sides of the field. .

3.Party Card Games

Header
Party Card Games

If space is limited and you’re having a smaller intimate gathering, why not consider card games? You can buy sets of card games such as Cards Against Humanity, What Do You Meme, and many others. These games are bound to get some laughs and start some interesting conversations. Alternatively, you can opt for traditional card games such as 21, poker, or spoons.

RULES :

Card games have entertained people for centuries and have always been a great way to pass time. They're social, portable and inexpensive (unless you're gambling with money), and the rules are easy to learn. .

4.Ring Toss

Header
Ring Toss

When looking for fun outdoor games that don’t require huge amounts of space, try ring toss! This is a classic party game that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. You can purchase a ring toss set or make your own using bottles and rings. The object of the game is to toss the ring onto the neck of the bottle. The player who gets the most rings on wins!.

RULES :

Player 1 tosses rings Begin by tossing all 5 of your colored rings toward the target attempting to land ringers around the pegs. Player 2 tosses rings The next player then takes their turn and tosses their rings towards the target. Players take score After tossing all 5 rings, all players total their points scored for the round. .

5.Pin the Tail on the Donkey

Header
Pin the Tail on the Donkey

For this party game, you will need a picture of some kind of animal or character. Each player is given a ‘tail’ and must try to place it as close as possible to the correct location on the image. The player who places their tail closest to the correct spot is the winner!.

RULES :

Hang a picture of a donkey without its tail on a wall. This serves as the target for the game. Make sure it’s at an appropriate height for the players.Hand the player a ‘tail’ and blindfold them. .
